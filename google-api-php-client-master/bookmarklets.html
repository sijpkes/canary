UoNLine Unlisted Video Bookmarklet

This bookmarklet assists you with embedding your unlisted YouTube videos by adding a link to your YouTube channel in the Blackboard Mashup tool.

Why use it?

  • If you don't want to share your Youtube video with the entire web.
  • If you want to pace students' access to video content during a course.

How do I use it?

  1. Drag the icon below to your Bookmarks/Favourites bar ...



    Youtube UoNline Personal Video Channel Access



  2. Get a google account
  3. Login to youtube
  4. Click Upload
  5. Select 'unlisted' from the dropdown
  6. Upload your video to youtube
  7. Select 'Advanced settings'
  8. Untick 'Notify subscribers'
  9. Click 'Done'
  10. Login to UoNline
  11. Navigate to your course and add a new item.
  12. While the Editor is open, click the bookmarklet
  13. A new item called 'Private Youtube Channel' will be added to the 'Mashups' dropdown
  14. Click it to access your video channel
  15. At this point you may be asked to authenticate. Click the link to do so, otherwise skip to the next step.
  16. Click the video you would like to embed
  17. That's it. You have now added an unlisted video to your UoNline course!

Note for users: You are not required to click the bookmarklet to embed an unlisted video each time you create an item in Blackboard. You have the option of embedding the item manually or using the standard mashup tool for public videos.
